## 2. Claude (Claude Pro)

**Many people's favorite model for writing and code.**

Anthropic's Claude is a clean, ad-free assistant that's a favorite for long-form writing and coding. Claude Pro is a polished experience.

**Strengths:**

- Excellent writing and reasoning, especially with Opus and Sonnet
- Calm, distraction-free interface
- Strong document analysis with a large context window

**Weaknesses:**

- **Anthropic models only** — no GPT, no Gemini, no Grok
- No native image generation
- Usage limits are famously opaque — heavy users hit caps without warning

**Pricing:** Claude Pro $20/mo; Max tiers at $100 and $200/mo for heavy users.

**Best for:** Writers and developers who are happy on Claude alone. ([Claude vs LLM Gateway Chat](https://chat.llmgateway.io/compare/claude).)

## 4. Perplexity

**Weaknesses:**

## 5. Microsoft Copilot

**AI woven into Windows and Office.**

Microsoft Copilot brings OpenAI models into Windows, Edge, and Microsoft 365, with deep ties to the Office apps.

**Strengths:**

- Built into Windows and Microsoft 365
- Strong for document and spreadsheet workflows
- Generous free tier

**Weaknesses:**

- Effectively OpenAI models only
- Most valuable inside the Microsoft ecosystem
- Less flexible than a dedicated multi-model app

**Pricing:** Free; Copilot now comes via Microsoft 365 Premium (~$20/mo) — standalone Copilot Pro is being retired for new users, with existing subscriptions supported until August 2026.

**Best for:** Microsoft 365 users who want AI inside the tools they already use.

## 6. Poe

**Many bots under one roof — but credit math to watch.**

Poe, from Quora, was an early multi-model aggregator. It hosts a wide range of bots and models behind a single subscription.

**Strengths:**

- Wide selection of models and community bots
- Single login for many models
- Mobile and web apps

**Weaknesses:**

- Per-message "compute points" make real usage hard to predict
- Heavier frontier models drain points fast
- Interface is bot-centric rather than a clean single chat

**Pricing:** Free tier; Premium $19.99/mo.

**Best for:** People who want to sample lots of models and bots and don't mind tracking points. ([Poe vs LLM Gateway Chat](https://chat.llmgateway.io/compare/poe).)
